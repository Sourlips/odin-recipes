Clam Chowder

Description

Clam chowder is a luxurious and indulgent soup that's sure to delight your taste buds. The rich and creamy base is infused with smoky bacon and sautéed aromatics, creating a depth of flavor that's both comforting and sophisticated. Tender chunks of clams and potatoes add a delightful texture to the soup, while the delicate brininess of the clams adds a subtle oceanic essence that's truly irresistible. The finishing touch of crispy bacon and fresh parsley elevate the dish to new heights, adding a burst of freshness and crunch that perfectly complements the velvety soup. Savor every spoonful of this decadent and elegant clam chowder, and indulge in the flavors of the sea.

ingredients

  • 4 slices bacon, chopped
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 celery stalks, chopped
  • 3 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1/4 cup all-purpose flour
  • 4 cups chicken or vegetable broth
  • 3 cups diced potatoes
  • 2 cups clam juice
  • 2 cups heavy cream
  • 2 (6.5 oz.) cans chopped clams, drained
  • Salt and black pepper to taste
  • Fresh parsley, chopped (optional)

instructions

  1. In a large pot, cook chopped bacon over medium heat until crispy. Remove bacon from the pot and set aside on a paper towel-lined plate.
  2. In the same pot, sauté onion, celery, and garlic in the bacon fat until onions are translucent.
  3. Add all-purpose flour to the pot and stir until vegetables are coated.
  4. Gradually add chicken or vegetable broth, stirring constantly until the mixture is smooth.
  5. Add diced potatoes and clam juice to the pot and bring to a boil. Reduce heat and simmer until potatoes are tender.
  6. Add heavy cream and chopped clams to the pot and stir to combine.
  7. Season with salt and black pepper to taste.
  8. Allow the chowder to simmer for a few more minutes to thicken and heat through.
  9. Serve the clam chowder hot, topped with crispy bacon and chopped parsley if desired.
