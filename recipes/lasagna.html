Lasagna

Description

Lasagna is a timeless Italian classic that exudes warmth and comfort in every bite. This rich and indulgent dish features layer upon layer of tender pasta sheets, creamy ricotta cheese, and savory tomato sauce, all nestled together in perfect harmony. The luxurious combination of cheeses, from the creamy ricotta to the nutty Parmesan and melty mozzarella, creates a velvety texture that melts in your mouth. Each bite is an explosion of flavor, with the tangy tomato sauce adding a depth of complexity that perfectly balances the richness of the cheeses. The aroma of fresh herbs and garlic wafts through the air, tantalizing your senses and heightening your anticipation for the first bite. With every forkful, you are transported to the rolling hills of Tuscany, where the flavors of Italy come alive in a symphony of taste. Lasagna is the ultimate comfort food, a dish that warms your soul and nourishes your spirit, and leaves you feeling satisfied and content.

ingredients

  • 12 lasagna noodles
  • 1 lb. ground beef
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 (28 oz.) can crushed tomatoes
  • 2 tbsp tomato paste
  • 1 tsp dried basil
  • 1 tsp dried oregano
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 16 oz. ricotta cheese
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 cup grated parmesan cheese
  • 1 egg
  • Fresh parsley, chopped (optional)

instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 375Â°F. Cook lasagna noodles according to package instructions, then rinse with cold water and set aside.
  2. In a large skillet, cook ground beef over medium-high heat until browned. Drain excess fat and add chopped onion and minced garlic. Cook until onions are translucent.
  3. Add crushed tomatoes, tomato paste, basil, oregano, salt, and pepper to the skillet with the beef. Simmer for 10 minutes.
  4. In a mixing bowl, combine ricotta cheese, 1 1/2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese, parmesan cheese, and egg. Mix well.
  5. Spread 1/2 cup of the meat sauce into the bottom of a 9x13 inch baking dish. Add a layer of lasagna noodles on top of the sauce.
  6. Spread half of the cheese mixture on top of the lasagna noodles. Repeat with another layer of meat sauce, lasagna noodles, and cheese mixture.
  7. Top with the remaining meat sauce and sprinkle the remaining 1/2 cup of shredded mozzarella cheese on top.
  8. Cover the baking dish with foil and bake in the oven for 25 minutes.
  9. Remove the foil and bake for an additional 25 minutes or until the cheese is melted and bubbly.
  10. Allow the lasagna to cool for a few minutes, then slice and serve. Garnish with chopped parsley if desired.
