Cup Of Noodle

Description

Cup Noodle, the instant noodle in a cup, is a beloved snack around the world. Created by Momofuku Ando in 1971, it revolutionized the instant noodle game by allowing people to enjoy hot, delicious noodles on the go. Ando was inspired to create Cup Noodle after seeing Americans enjoying their soup out of cups. He thought, "Why not put noodles in a cup, too?" The rest is history.

Over the years, Cup Noodle has become an iconic snack, loved by everyone from college students to busy professionals. It's the perfect solution for those times when you're too lazy to cook or when you're just craving something salty and delicious. Plus, who can resist peeling back the lid and revealing those steamy noodles? It's like a mini adventure in every cup.

ingredients

  • 1 Cup Noodle
  • 2 teaspoons of regret for not cooking a proper meal
  • 1 dash of nostalgia for your broke college days
  • 1 pinch of procrastination for when you should be doing something else
  • 1 sprinkle of sweatpants you've been wearing for three days
  • 1 tablespoon of delusion that this is a balanced meal
  • 1 dash of denial that you'll probably eat this again tomorrow

instructions

  1. "Free the noodles": Take your Cup Noodle out of its package and set it free from its cardboard prison. Remove the lid and sprinkle in the seasoning to give your noodles some flavor love.
  2. "Add hot water drops: Add boiling water to the fill line and watch the magic happen
  3. "Twirl and slurp": Use your fork to twirl and swirl the noodles into submission. Slurp each strand like it's the best thing you've ever tasted and savor the deliciousness.
