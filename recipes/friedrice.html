Fried Rice

Description

Homemade fried rice is a classic comfort food that can be enjoyed on its own or as a side dish to complement any meal. The great thing about this dish is that it can be made with simple ingredients that you likely already have in your kitchen. Our recipe for homemade fried rice features fluffy rice, savory vegetables, tender chicken, and the perfect balance of seasonings to create a flavorful and satisfying meal. The rice is first cooked to perfection and then saut√©ed with vegetables and chicken in a hot wok to impart a smoky flavor and crispy texture. The dish is finished with a drizzle of soy sauce, giving it an umami-rich taste that will leave you craving for more. Whether you're looking for a quick weeknight dinner or a comforting meal on a cold day, this homemade fried rice recipe is sure to become a family favorite.

ingredients

  • Cooked rice (preferably day-old)
  • Eggs (beaten)
  • Chicken (diced or shredded)
  • Vegetables (such as peas, carrots, onions, and green onions)
  • Soy sauce
  • Sesame oil
  • Black pepper

Recipe

  1. Cook the rice according to the package instructions and set it aside to cool. It's best to use day-old rice for this recipe as it will have a better texture.
  2. In a wok or a large skillet, heat some vegetable oil over high heat. Once the oil is hot, add the diced chicken and cook until it's no longer pink.
  3. Push the chicken to the sides of the wok and add some minced garlic to the center. Stir the garlic until fragrant, then add the beaten eggs and scramble them until they are fully cooked.
  4. Mix the cooked chicken, garlic, and eggs together in the wok.
  5. Add your choice of vegetables, such as peas, carrots, onions, or green onions. Stir-fry the vegetables for a few minutes until they are tender.
  6. Add the cooled rice to the wok and stir everything together until the rice is well combined with the other ingredients.
  7. Add soy sauce and sesame oil to the wok, and continue to stir everything together.
  8. Season with salt and black pepper to taste.
  9. Once everything is heated through and well combined, turn off the heat and serve your homemade fried rice hot with your favorite protein or enjoy it on its own as a vegetarian dish.
